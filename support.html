Why we need your support

FAST Wiki was built to solve a problem many students face: scattered information. Admissions, merit, academic rules, finances, campus life, and career guidance all exist somewhere, but finding clear answers often takes more time than it should.

We spent countless hours collecting information, organizing pages, improving the design, testing links, adding search functionality, and making the platform easier to use. The website is free for everyone, but keeping it online still has costs like domain renewal and future improvements.
