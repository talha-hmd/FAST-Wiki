About Us

Our Mission

Getting into FAST is a dream for many, but the application process from entry test criteria to merit calculations is often confusing and lacks clear, centralized guidance. Thousands of applicants enter the process every year with questions that aren't answered by the official portal.

Our mission is to bridge that gap. We created FAST Wiki to be the resource we wish we had: a transparent, student-led, and comprehensive guide for everyone thinking about, or currently applying to, FAST. We’ve synthesized the confusion into clear paths so your only concern is performing your best on test day, not figuring out the application system.

The Founders

Muhammad Talha Hamid

Getting into FAST in 2024 was a journey but not an easy one. Like many, I started with zero roadmap and even less guidance on how to navigate the complex application process. About a year later, I started a WhatsApp community to address this issue and that quickly grew to over 1,000+ members.

Later on, realizing that manual support wasn't scalable, I partnered with Ahmed to build this platform. I’ve poured every lesson learned from thousands of applicant queries into this site, creating the resource I wish I had on day one. I hope this wiki makes your journey to FAST a little bit clearer.
